## Get Involved

rqlite is an open-source project with an active community of users and contributors. Here's how you can get involved:

### Join the Conversation

- **[Slack](https://www.rqlite.io/join-slack)** - Chat with other rqlite users and the development team in real-time
- **[Google Group](https://groups.google.com/g/rqlite)** - Join the mailing list for discussions, announcements, and help
- **[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/rqlite/rqlite/discussions)** - Ask questions and share ideas

### Office Hours

Want to talk directly with the rqlite team? [Book a time](https://rqlite.io/office-hours) for a one-on-one conversation about your use case, architecture questions, or anything rqlite-related.

### Contribute

rqlite welcomes contributions of all kinds:

- **[Report Issues](https://github.com/rqlite/rqlite/issues)** - Found a bug or have a feature request? Let us know
- **[Submit Pull Requests](https://github.com/rqlite/rqlite/pulls)** - Code contributions are always appreciated
- **[Improve Documentation](https://github.com/rqlite/rqlite.io)** - Help make the docs clearer and more comprehensive

### Stay Updated

- **[Blog](https://www.philipotoole.com/tag/rqlite/)** - Read about rqlite development, best practices, and use cases
- **[GitHub Releases](https://github.com/rqlite/rqlite/releases)** - Get notified about new releases

### Contact

Have questions or want to discuss a potential use case? [Contact the rqlite team](https://www.philipotoole.com/contact/).
